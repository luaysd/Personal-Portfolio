Here's a time line of Diego Maradona's life:
  • 1960 - Born in Lanús, Argentina
  • 1968 - Spotted by a talent scout while he was playing
    • in his neighbourhood club Estrella Roja
  • 1976 - Made his professional debut for Argentinos Juniors,
  • 1982 - Transferred to Barcelona in Spain for a then world record fee of £5 million
  • 1984 - Maradona arrived in Naples and was presented to the world media
    • as a Napoli player where he reached the peak of his career
  • 1992 - Left Napoli serving a 15-month ban for failing a drug test for cocaine
  • 2000 - Officially retired from professional football
  • 2020 - Suffered a heart attack and died after a brain sugery
